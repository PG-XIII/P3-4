How to use:

1. Click on the canvas on the positions you want to set a point

2. Drag a point around to change it's position

3. Change the degree of the curve using the dropdown above

4. The green curve is the graph of the function calculated by the Least-Squares Method, and the blue curve is the bézier curve generated from that function.

5. The red crosses are the control points of the bézier curve.
